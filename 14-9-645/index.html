Department of Mathematics and Statistics

AMAT 645: Intro to Algebraic Topology, Fall 2014

Instructor

Prof. Marco Varisco, mvarisco@albany.edu, www.albany.edu/~mv312143/
Office: ES-120C, Office Hours: by appointment.

Schedule

MWF 11:30–12:25 in ES-153.

Prerequisites

AMAT 520A & 540B, or permission of instructor.

Description

  • Basic category theory notions — categories, functors, natural transformations, adjunctions, (co)limits, (co)ends, Kan extensions — and lots of examples;
  • the relation among small categories, simplicial sets, and topological spaces;
  • monoidal categories and enriched category theory;
  • time permitting, some higher category theory at the end.

Textbook

None, but lecture notes and additional reading material will be distributed in class.

Grading

Weekly homework assignments.
